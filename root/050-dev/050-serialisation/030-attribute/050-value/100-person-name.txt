title: person-name
link_as: keyword
--

A person's name, containing the various fields and a cultural identifier.


h3(property). type

@"person-name"@


h3(property). readable

A formatted representation of this name, taking into account the cultural presentation.


h3(property). value

A dictionary containing the fields of this value. All are optional, except there must be at least one value.


h3(property). value.first

First name.


h3(property). value.middle

Middle name.


h3(property). value.last

Last name / surname / family name.


h3(property). value.suffix

Suffix (eg 'III')


h3(property). value.title

Title (eg 'Ms')


h3(property). value.culture

Culture of name: @"western"@, @"western_list"@, @"eastern"@.
