"Handlebars helpers":http://handlebarsjs.com/block_helpers.html augment the features available for rendering a template.

You can also specify your own helpers for your plugin using [node:plugin/misc/handlebars#registerHandlebarsHelper] or supply them in the options to a Template's [node:plugin/interface/template#render] function.
