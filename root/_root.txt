The Haplo Platform offers the ability to build powerful applications for managing "real world" information that doesn't fit into a conventional database.

This is the technical documentation for Haplo, describing how to [node:setup:set up and evolve] a Haplo application, and how to [node:dev/plugin:develop plugins] and connect it to [node:dev/xml-http:external systems].

As you're probably dealing with confidential information in your Haplo application, you must read our [node:security].
